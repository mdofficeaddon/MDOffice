# Nested Lists Showcase

This document demonstrates improved nested list styling in HTML exports.

## Regular Bullet Lists

### Basic Nesting
- Top level item 1
- Top level item 2
  - Second level item A
  - Second level item B
    - Third level item i
    - Third level item ii
      - Fourth level item 1
      - Fourth level item 2
        - Fifth level item a
        - Fifth level item b

  - Subcategory A1
    - Detail A1.1
    - Detail A1.2
  - Subcategory A2
    - Detail A2.1
    - Detail A2.2
